Model Rocket Project

My initial CAD of the rocket, with calculated stability.

I ran a simulation on the expected flight data, using the recorded NOAA temperature, windspeed, air pressure, altitude, and coordinates.

I built and then painted the rocket to closely match the MBDA Meteor. The rocket was then launched.
